About Us

My Camping Adventure is all about finding new opportunities and experiences. We strive to make camping stress-free and enjoyable. Our site contains detailed guides and lists for many different types of camping adventures and we are proud to support a community of campers looking to interact with others.


Camping is about exploring and relaxing so My Camping Adventure is here to cater to anyone who wants to dip their toes in for the first time or seasoned experts embarking on their 100th camping trip. Our website hosts events for all ages and needs, we exhibit a welcoming and warm spirit to everyone!


Join today to gain access to community events, comprehensive camping guides and detailed shopping and checklists. View our map to find new spots or just to explore all the possibilities that lie out there waiting to be enjoyed.


For more information feel free to contact us!
