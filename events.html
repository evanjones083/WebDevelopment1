Welcome to the event center!

Events are hosted by the community and members. To create a new event, fill out the event form.

Hike the Mountain!

Location: Rocky Mountain Park

Join us for a hiking trip! The hike will be split into 3 parts and you can participate in any part or all of them. We will be following the trails and I suggest bringing the items from the hiking shopping list located in the lists section. Let's make this year's hiking trip the biggest yet!

Animal and Plant seeing trip

Location: To be decided

New camping guides are looking to test their skills by leading small groups in search of various plant and animal life. On this trip you will learn how to identify different forms of trees, plants, and animal markings. This event will only last a few hours but anyone is welcome to join in on the fun!

River Float 2023

Location: Flint River Park

This year's river float will be starting at Sprewell Bluff and will continue past the bridge. Anyone who wants to join can, and we would like a large group for this years float. Bring anything you want and I suggest bringing your own floats or kayaks as the campground will only have a limited number to rent out.

Cool nights in the wilderness

Location: Riverbend Camping Ground

A 2 night camping extravaganza has been planned for the Riverbend campground. We do this every 2 years and it is always a great time so feel free to join in! Half of the campground is available to anyone who signs up for this event.
